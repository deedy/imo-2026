# Raw Cartesian and complex elimination

## Idea
Normalize $A=0$, $B=2$, write all points in coordinates, and eliminate the side length and the direction of $AC$ directly from the angle and incidence conditions. The target is equality of the powers of $M$ and $N$ with respect to $(AKL)$.

## Status
Abandoned as a final presentation. The method verifies the theorem, but direct elimination expands into long expressions and hides the geometric role of the last incidence condition. Its useful coordinate parametrization was retained in the completed invariant-factorization approach.

# Complex unit-exponential factorization

## Idea
Set $X=e^{ix}$, $Y=e^{iy}$, $Z=e^{iz}$ and express conjugation by $X\mapsto X^{-1}$, and similarly for $Y,Z$. The coordinates of $K,L,N$ become rational functions of $X,Y,Z$ and the ray parameter. The quotient of the power determinant by the $CK$ incidence determinant factors into the scalar appearing in the final lemma.

# Altitude / right-angle first observation

## Idea
From any triangle, Mulan can cut along an altitude from a suitable vertex (taking the foot in the interior of the opposite side) so that both children have a right angle. Hence $\theta=90^\circ$ is winning. More generally, if a retained triangle has an angle $m\theta$, it can be split into $\theta$ and $(m-1)\theta$, suggesting the family $\theta=90^\circ/n$.

## Limitation
This is not the complete family. For example, $\theta=60^\circ$ also works, but cannot be obtained from the altitude observation. The stronger normalized-angle argument replaces the special right angle $90^\circ$ by the fact that the total $180^\circ$ is an integral multiple of $\theta$.

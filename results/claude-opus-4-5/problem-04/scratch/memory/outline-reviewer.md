# outline-reviewer per-role rules

ALWAYS: For game-theory problems, verify computationally that claimed constructions (e.g., valid cuts) exist for representative cases (because manual interval/condition analysis is error-prone, round 2)

ALWAYS: For bidirectional characterization problems, check whether single-direction approaches are presented as whole attempts -- they should be flagged as incomplete unless paired with their complement (because CLAUDE.md requires each slug to target the problem's actual claim, round 2)

ALWAYS: When reviewing cascade/iteration strategies, verify the invariant/monovariant actually persists through adversary choices, not just through the protagonist's ideal path (because the adversary can disrupt steering assumptions, round 2)
